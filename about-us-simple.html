About Us

Utricle Studios Limited is a full service media production company with a focus on design, animation and creative technology.
We are a diverse team of experts in pre-production, production and post-production using conceptualisation, storyboards, matte painting, character and motion design, visual effects for large format films, TV production, commercials and integrated media.

Over the years, we have worked with exceptional partners to Imagine and Create. We are telling the stories that matter and with every piece of work we are creating magic for education and entertainment.

With a collection of over 250 productions comprising of explainer videos, short films and documentaries, we are currently producing an array of educational TV series and are set to premiere our animated show, Cluck Stars and a feature length 3D production, Gammy and the Living Things.
